Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about HerdCycle and how it can help your operation.

How difficult is it to get started with HerdCycle?

We've designed HerdCycle to be intuitive and easy to use, even for those who aren't tech-savvy. Our quick-start guides and import tools make it simple to get your existing data into the system. Plus, our dedicated support team is always available to help with the transition.

Can I use HerdCycle offline in the field?

Yes! Our mobile app works offline, allowing you to record information in the field without internet connection. Once you're back in range, the data automatically syncs to your account, ensuring all your records stay up-to-date.

How secure is my farm data?

Your data security is our top priority. HerdCycle uses bank-level encryption to protect your information, and we never share or sell your data to third parties. You maintain complete ownership and control over all your farm records.

Do I need special equipment to use HerdCycle?

No special equipment is required. HerdCycle works with standard smartphones, tablets, and computers. However, it does integrate seamlessly with RFID readers, scales, and other ranch technology if you choose to use them for more automated data collection.
